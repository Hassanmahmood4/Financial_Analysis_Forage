10-K Financial Analysis Report

Source: SEC EDGAR 10-K filings for Microsoft, Tesla, and Apple, fiscal years 2023 to 2025.

Key Findings

In fiscal 2025, Apple had the highest revenue at $416.16B.

From 2023 to 2025, Microsoft had the strongest total revenue growth at 32.94%.

Microsoft showed broad-based growth, Apple remained highly profitable, and Tesla showed the greatest profitability pressure.
